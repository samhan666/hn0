**ISP Proxies (Static Residential Proxies)**

ISP Proxies, also referred to as Static Residential Proxies, represent a hybrid solution that combines the legitimacy of residential IPs with the stability and speed of datacenter infrastructure. These are real residential IP addresses that are hosted on servers within data centers, offering consistent IP addresses for extended periods. Bright Data's ISP proxy network comprises over 700,000 IPs, providing a reliable solution for tasks that require persistent sessions and a high trust score without the frequent rotation of standard residential proxies . Key applications include managing multiple social media accounts, brand protection, continuous monitoring, and any scenario where maintaining a consistent online identity is crucial. They support both HTTP/S and SOCKS5 protocols, offering flexibility for various integration needs .

**Billing Logic and Payment Methods**

Bright Data's billing cycle commences on the 1st of each month. Monthly commitment payments are automatically charged on this date for active accounts. If a user joins mid-month, the first payment covers the full monthly commitment, but usage is retroactively applied only for the active days, with any unused portion rolling over as credit for the subsequent month .

Bandwidth Calculation: Bandwidth is calculated as the sum of data transmitted to and from the target site, including request headers, request data (for POST requests), response headers, and response data. Trial usage is not counted towards billing .

Usage Limits: Users can set daily usage limits based on bandwidth (bytes) or money spent (dollars) for each proxy zone. Once a limit is reached, the zone is automatically suspended, providing cost control .

Payment Methods: Bright Data supports a wide range of payment options, including PayPal, Payoneer, Alipay, Google Pay, wire transfers, and major credit cards .

KYC Process: For Residential and Mobile IP networks, a Know Your Customer (KYC) process is required. This involves a short compliance verification, including a potential video call and company/personal information verification, to ensure ethical use and prevent abuse of the network .
